Dr. Niranjan Hiranandani

Dr. Niranjan Hiranandani, a 1st generation entrepreneur, is renowned as one of India’s most influential business leaders and a visionary within the real estate sector. As the Co-Founder of Hiranandani Group, he has redefined urban development in India, pioneering the concept of incorporated townships and placing benchmarks in sustainable, and eco-friendly construction.

Born and raised in Mumbai, Dr. Hiranandani’s journey reflects resilience, innovation, and a determination to excellence. With a sturdy academic foundation as a Chartered Accountant, he began his profession with modest beginnings but quickly recognized the untapped capability in the real estate industry. His early tasks, which include the iconic Hiranandani Gardens in Powai, transformed underdeveloped areas into thriving urban hubs, revolutionizing India’s urban landscape.

Dr. Hiranandani’s impact extends beyond real estate. A thought leader and recommended for policy reforms, he has been instrumental in shaping India’s housing and infrastructure policies. His contributions to organization agencies, which include the National Real Estate Development Council (NAREDCO), replicate his dedication to advancing the arena while making sure its alignment with sustainable development goals.

Equally devoted to philanthropy, Dr. Hiranandani has championed tasks in education, healthcare, and community development. His legacy is not described through architectural landmarks but additionally through the use of his enduring impact on society, inspiring future generations to combine commercial enterprise acumen with social responsibility.

Dr. Niranjan Hiranandani’s leadership, vision, and values set him aside as a transformative figure in Indian real estate and beyond.
