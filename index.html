About

Dr. Niranjan Hiranandani is a first-generation entrepreneur and one of India’s most respected real estate visionaries. His integrated township developments have reshaped urban infrastructure standards across Mumbai, Chennai, Panvel and emerging corridors.

Known for architectural consistency, infrastructure planning precision, and premium positioning, his developments blend residential, commercial, retail and social infrastructure within self-sustained ecosystems.

Beyond real estate development, Dr. Niranjan Hiranandani is an influential industry voice, having served as Chairman of National Real Estate Development Council (NAREDCO) and held leadership roles at Associated Chambers of Commerce and Industry of India (ASSOCHAM). Through policy advocacy, thought leadership, and large-scale township expansion across key Indian growth corridors, he continues to shape the future of infrastructure and housing reform. His journey as a first-generation Indian business leader reflects strategic foresight, execution capability, and a sustained contribution to India’s urban transformation.
